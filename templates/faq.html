Frequently Asked Questions

For all your questions. Use these questions & answers
to better understand how the tool functions and the different capabilites.

  • How do I search for a song's lyrics?

    • You will need to input a song and an artist in the form on the home page. You will be redirected to a lyric's page.

  • How do I search the songs of a given album?

    • You will need to input an album and an artist in the form on the home page. You will be redirected to a list of songs for that album.

  • How do I search the discography of an artist?

    • You will need to input an artist in the form on the home page. You will be redirected to a list of albums from the given artist.
